    Task Assigner Dashboard Information

    Welcome to the Task Assigner Dashboard!

    This dashboard allows you to manage tasks effectively by providing an overview of tasks assigned by you and tasks assigned to you.

    Features:
    • Assign Task: Click on the "ASSIGN TASK" button to assign tasks to other users. You will enter task details and select the user to assign it to.
    • View Tasks Assigned by You: This section displays all tasks you have assigned to others, including their titles, content, assigned users, and deadlines. You can update or delete these tasks.
    • View Tasks Assigned to You: This section shows tasks assigned to you by others. You can see task details and mark tasks as completed.
    • Logout: Always remember to log out of your account by clicking the "LOGOUT" button.
    How to Use:
    1. Log in: Access the dashboard after logging in with your username and password.
    2. Assign a New Task: Click the "ASSIGN TASK" button and fill in the task details, including the user to whom you're assigning the task.
    3. Manage Assigned Tasks: Under the "Tasks Assigned by You" section, view, update, or delete tasks that you have assigned. Use the provided buttons to perform these actions.
    4. Track Tasks Assigned to You: In the "Tasks Assigned to You" section, view tasks assigned to you. Click "Completed Assigned Task" to mark tasks as complete.
    5. Log Out: When you're finished, click the "LOGOUT" button to securely exit your account.
