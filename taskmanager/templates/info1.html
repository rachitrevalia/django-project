    Task Manager Dashboard Information

    Welcome to the Task Manager Dashboard!

    This dashboard is designed to help you manage your tasks efficiently. Below you will find information on how to navigate and use the features of this application.

    Features:
    • Create Task: Click on the "CREATE TASK" button to add a new task. You will be prompted to enter the task title, content, and deadline.
    • Task Assigner: Use the "TASK ASSIGNER" button to assign tasks to other users. You can select users from the list and assign them specific tasks.
    • View Tasks: Your tasks will be displayed in a grid format on the dashboard. Each task card shows the title, content, and deadline. You can update or delete tasks using the buttons on each card.
    • Logout: Click the "LOGOUT" button to securely log out of your account.
    How to Use:
    1. Log in: Access the dashboard after logging in with your username and password.
    2. Create a New Task: Click the "CREATE TASK" button and fill in the required fields. Save your task to see it on your dashboard.
    3. Assign Tasks: To assign tasks to other users, click on the "TASK ASSIGNER" button. Select the task and the user you want to assign it to.
    4. Manage Your Tasks: View your tasks on the dashboard. Click "Update Task" to edit details or "Delete Task" to remove it.
    5. Log Out: When you're done, remember to log out by clicking the "LOGOUT" button.
